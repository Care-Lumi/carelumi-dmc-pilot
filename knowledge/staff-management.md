# Staff Management

## Overview
The Staff page manages non-provider employees including nurses, surgical techs, administrative staff, and their credentials, training, and compliance requirements.

## Staff List View

### Displayed Information
- **Staff Name**: Full name with photo
- **Role**: RN, LVN, Surgical Tech, Admin, etc.
- **Department**: Assignment
- **Status**: Active, Inactive, LOA
- **License Status**: Current, Expiring Soon, Expired
- **Training Compliance**: Percentage complete
- **Health Screening**: Current, Due Soon, Overdue
- **Next Expiration**: Soonest expiring item

### Filters
- Role/Position
- Department
- Facility
- License Status
- Training Compliance
- Hire Date Range
- Employment Status

### Bulk Actions
- Send Training Reminders
- Generate Competency Reports
- Export Staff List
- Update Department
- Schedule Training
