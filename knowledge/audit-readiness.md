# Audit Readiness

## Overview
The Audit Readiness page provides a comprehensive view of your organization's compliance status and helps you prepare for regulatory surveys, accreditation surveys, and internal audits.

## Audit Readiness Dashboard

### Overall Compliance Score
- Real-time compliance percentage
- Calculation based on all requirements
- Trend over time (30/60/90 days)
- Comparison to previous periods
- Goal setting and tracking

### Score Breakdown
Compliance scored across:
- **Provider Credentials** (25%)
- **Facility Licenses** (20%)
- **Staff Compliance** (20%)
- **Policies & Procedures** (15%)
- **Training & Competencies** (10%)
- **Quality & Safety** (10%)

### Critical Gaps
- High-priority compliance issues
- Regulatory violations
- Expired credentials
- Missing required documents
- Incomplete files
- Action items with owners and due dates

### Expiring Soon
- Items expiring in next 90 days
- Prioritized by importance
- Direct links to renew/update
- Bulk action capabilities
- Reminder settings

## Audit Preparation

### Survey Readiness Assessment
- Run comprehensive compliance check
- Identifies gaps by standard
- Generates priority action list
- Estimates time to compliance
- Creates preparation timeline

### Pre-Survey Checklist
**30 Days Before:**
- Review all policies for currency
- Check all licenses current
- Verify staff training complete
- Review quality data
- Check environment of care
- Prepare document binders

**7 Days Before:**
- Mock tracer patients
- Staff education on survey process
- Review common findings
- Prepare conference room
- Assemble all required documents
- Test internet/systems access

**Day of Survey:**
- Opening conference checklist
- Document tracking system
- Daily briefing schedule
- Communication protocol
- Closing conference prep

### Mock Survey Tools
- Tracer methodology tools
- Staff interview guides
- Environment of care checklist
- Document review checklist
- Scoring guides
- Findings documentation
