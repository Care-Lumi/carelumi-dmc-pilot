# Facilities Management

## Overview
The Facilities page manages facility licenses, accreditations, insurance, and compliance requirements for all locations in your organization.

## Facility List View

### Displayed Information
- **Facility Name**: Full facility name
- **Type**: ASC, Hospital, Clinic, Office
- **Address**: Location
- **License Status**: Current, Expiring Soon, Expired
- **Accreditation**: AAAHC, AAASF, Joint Commission
- **Active Providers**: Count of credentialed providers
- **Compliance Score**: Overall compliance percentage
- **Next Expiration**: Soonest expiring credential

### Quick Actions
- Add New Facility
- Export Facility List
- Generate Compliance Report
- View Expiring Items
