# Staff Training and Education Requirements

## Overview
All ASC staff must receive appropriate training and continuing education to maintain competency and ensure patient safety.

## Initial Orientation

### All Staff
- Facility policies and procedures
- Patient rights and confidentiality
- Infection control practices
- Emergency procedures and evacuation
- Fire safety and equipment use
- OSHA bloodborne pathogens training

### Clinical Staff Additional Topics
- Patient assessment procedures
- Medication administration protocols
- Documentation requirements
- Equipment operation
- Sterile technique
- Patient care standards

## Annual Required Training

### Mandatory for All Staff
- Fire safety (annually)
- Emergency procedures (annually)
- Infection control (annually)
- HIPAA compliance (annually)
- Workplace safety (annually)

### Clinical Staff Additional Requirements
- CPR/BLS certification (every 2 years)
- ACLS certification (if required by role)
- Specialty-specific continuing education
- Competency assessments

## Continuing Education

### Nursing Staff
- 20 hours per 2-year license renewal period (Texas requirement)
- Must include applicable specialty topics
- May include online or in-person courses

### Physicians
- Must maintain board certification (if applicable)
- CME requirements vary by specialty
- Documentation must be on file

## Competency Assessment
- Initial competency evaluation required
- Annual competency verification
- Skills validation for new procedures or equipment
- Documentation of all assessments

## Documentation Requirements
- Training attendance records
- Certificates of completion
- Competency assessment forms
- License and certification copies
- Annual evaluation of education needs

## Training Resources
- Online training platforms
- Professional associations
- State and federal agencies
- Equipment manufacturer training
- Internal expert staff

## Best Practices
- Maintain training calendar
- Send reminders before expirations
- Keep digital copies of all certificates
- Track compliance in centralized system
- Review training effectiveness annually
